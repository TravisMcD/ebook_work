Table of contents: Allows you to navigate to a specific chapter/page within the book.
Notes: Notes allow you to save ideas as you read a page. Click the button to activate this tool. Then point and click on the spot where you want to add a note. The note textbox will displayed. Click inside the textbox and input your note. Click Save to save the note and close the box. Notes are saved to the computer at which you're working.
Glossary: Click the Glossary icon to load the glossary, then click a letter. You will see a list of terms starting with that letter from which to choose. Click the term to see a definition. Translations are provided in English and Spanish.
Bookmark: The Bookmark tool allows you to continue reading from the page you last read. Click on the book mark icon and fill in the title and description. Then click the add button. To open a page you have bookmarked, click on Bookmark, Bookmarked Pages and select the page you wish to start from.
Highlight: Just like the traditional highlighter you can highlight any part of a page. Click the button to activate the tool. Then select the material you whould like to highlight. Roll over the highlighting and click close button to remove it.
Zoom In: Zooms into the page 10%. You can zoom in up to 130%.
Zoom Out: Zooms out of the page by 10%. You can reduce the view back to 100%.
Search: Search allows you to look for any word or pharse in the book. Click the Search icon, type your search term into the text box and press enter. Click a red page number to navigate to that page of the book. Search results are displayed in order of relevance.
Navigation: Use the forward arrow to go to the next page, and the back arrow to go to the previous page in the book.
