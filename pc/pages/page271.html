Plant Pigments And Photosynthesis

and manufacture food. In other words, it uses solar energy (light) to produce chemical energy 
(carbohydrates). Photosynthesis consumes carbon dioxide (CO

All photosynthetic reactions are either light-dependent or light-independent. Both types occur in special-
