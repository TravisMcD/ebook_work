Yeast cells contain catalase, an enzyme which 
helps convert hydrogen peroxide to water and ox-
ygen. This enzyme is very significant as hydrogen 
peroxide can be toxic to cells if allowed to accu-
mulate. The effect of catalase can be seen when 
yeast is combined with hydrogen peroxide 
(Catalase: 2 H2O2 2 H2O + O2 ).

In this experiment, you will examine the effects of 
temperature on enzyme (catalase) activity based 
on the amount of oxygen produced. Note, be sure 
to remain observant for effervescence when ana-
lyzing your results.
