Table 4: Sordaria fimicola Crossover Data 

The measurement to describe the difference between genes is referred to as map unit. As the distance in-
creases between genes, the likelihood of these genes crossing over independently becomes greater, there-
fore demonstrating that the proportion of crossovers corresponds with the distance between genes. As a rule, 
the percentage of recombinations is equal to the number of map units between two genes or a gene and the 
centromere.
