Time and Materials 

Lab 23  Ecological Interactions 

Time Required: 2 hours (plus 7 additional days of observation)  

Additional Materials: Large pitcher/jug/glass (capable of holding 1000 
mL), hot water bath, tap water, outdoor water source, and paper towels. 

 

Lab 24   Environmental Succession  

Time Required: 2 hours (plus 4 - 8 weeks for microbial incubation and 
observation)  

Additional Materials: 2 clean and clear two liter plastic bottles, 8 - 10 
cups of dirt/mud, 8 - 10 cups of water, large bowl, light source, and 
scissors.
